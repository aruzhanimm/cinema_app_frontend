1. Information We Collect

Personal Information

We collect information that you provide directly to us when you:

  • Create an account
  • Subscribe to our services
  • Contact our support team
  • Participate in surveys or promotions

Automatically Collected Information

When you use our services, we automatically collect:

  • Device information (IP address, browser type, operating system)
  • Usage data (movies watched, search queries, viewing time)
  • Location data (general geographic location)
  • Cookies and similar technologies

2. How We Use Your Data

We use the information we collect to:

Service Delivery

Recommend content based on your preferences

Security

Protect against fraud and unauthorized access

Communication

Send service updates and promotional offers

Our services are not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have collected information from a child under 13, please contact us immediately.
